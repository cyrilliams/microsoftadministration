# E5 Licensing

I'm going to briefly go over Microsoft E5 and Office E5 licenses in this doc.

**Office E5**

Covers productivity apps like Word, Excel, Teams, etc

**Microsoft E5**

Covers Micrsoft features as a whole; such as productivity apps as well Entra, Intune and Windows licenses.
